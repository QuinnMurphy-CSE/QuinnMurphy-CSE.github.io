About me

As a Freshman Computer Science student at the University of South Carolina, I have learned more about the important role that technology holds in society. My eyes have been widened towards the importance of ethics and sustainability and the technological world. My mission as a computer scientist is to oversee the development of both existing and new technologies that will prove to benefit humanity. I aim to do so using sustainable work practices and focusing on both accessible and ethical solutions to real-world problems. I hope to make the average quality of life of people around the world rise, without putting a substantial strain on the Earth and jobs. I will approach my future career with an open mind, full of innovation, responsibility, and determination. Three characteristics that will prove to strengthen not only my leadership but also my teamwork capabilities while I work towards a positive contribution to the world. When looking towards to future, I envision myself overseeing and managing projects that are innovating new technologies and adapting existing technologies for the world to come. Putting technology to use in fields where its potential is not yet being fully utilized and teaching rising computer scientists about the importance of their job.
